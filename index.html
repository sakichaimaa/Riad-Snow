RIAD SNOW ABOUT INFO

In the heart of snowy nature, Riad Snow delivers a unique hotel experience that blends the serenity of alpine peaks with the charm of Moroccan details. Designed in black, burgundy, and grey, the hotel embodies a refined identity that reflects both luxury and warmth, turning every stay into a complete sensory journey.

Guests can enjoy a private cinema for unforgettable adventures, a modern fitness center to stay active, a traditional Moroccan hammam for deep relaxation, and two distinctive restaurants: an authentic Moroccan venue serving refined traditional dishes, and an elegant European restaurant offering a global culinary experience.

From the serene library inviting guests to dive into knowledge, to the lush gardens offering moments of reflection and tranquility, every corner of the riad is crafted to provide exceptional experiences of comfort and inspiration. With a professional team delivering personalized service and warm hospitality, the stay becomes an unforgettable journey, where every guest feels at home amidst the snow.
